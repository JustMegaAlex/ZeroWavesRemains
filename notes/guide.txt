Controls:

     WASD - move

     Left mouse - shoot

     Right mouse - boost

     Scroll mouse - change zoom

     Q/E - switch weapon

     1...3 - also switch weapon

     P - pause/unpause

     F - choose/interact with items and shop

How to interact with items and shop:

     1. Fly close to an item
     2. See it being highlighted
     3. Press F to interact
